Swiss Home Exchange – Summer 2024

Home Exchange: Beautiful Swiss Village (Unterägeri)

We are an international family of four (2 adults, 2 kids – a 16-year-old girl and a 13-year-old boy) looking to exchange our home in Switzerland for 1-4 weeks from mid-July to mid-August 2024. We’re interested in a home exchange with families in the San Francisco Bay Area, Peninsula, Silicon Valley or Berkeley, Rockridge area, or Los Angeles (preferably Marina del Rey or other neighborhoods) and possibly San Diego.

Our Home & Location

We live in a spacious, ground-floor 4 bedroom, 2 bathroom apartment (sleeps 8-10) in the picturesque Swiss village of Unterägeri, located in the canton of Zug. Our apartment has an open-plan kitchen, living, and dining area. We also have a garden with a direct access to playgrounds in a lovely, peaceful, child-friendly area.The location is a perfect base to explore Switzerland via train, driving, hiking or biking.

  • A beautiful lake, 14 km all around, perfect for walks and water activities.
  • Forests, mountains, and scenic bike & hike trails right at your doorstep.
  • 10 minutes' drive to Zug, 45 minutes to Zurich, and 45 minutes to Luzern.
  • Cruise around the lake on a boat.
  • We have a beautiful badi or lido or beach on the lake, 5 minutes walk to the lake but its enclosed with a diving board, slides, stand-up paddles etc.
  • Horse back riding, mountain or road bike riding to Zugerberg or Wildspitz or around the lake.
  • 10 minutes drive to Sattel Hochstuckli ski resort and home to Skywalk suspension bride, Gondelbahn etc.
  • An ideal base for exploring central Switzerland or simply relaxing in a peaceful, nature-filled environment.
  • Zurich to central Paris - 4 hours train ride, Zug to central Milan 3 hour train ride, 1 hour drive to Konstanz, Germany, 2 hours to Basel, French border, 1.5 hours to Austrian border and an hour to Vaduz, Liechtenstein
